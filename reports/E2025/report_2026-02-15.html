What we measure & why it matters

We track more than box-score totals because production quality and role context usually explain future games better than single-game points. Efficiency metrics such as TS and eFG tell us whether scoring came from sustainable shot value (threes, free throws, and clean attempts) rather than volume alone.

Usage_proxy shows offensive responsibility. A player with high TS on very low usage may simply be finishing easy possessions, while a high-usage player with average TS can still be driving team offense. Reading efficiency together with usage helps separate true creators from low-volume outliers.

GoG deltas highlight short-term shocks from one game to the next, which is useful for spotting role changes, injury impacts, or matchup swings quickly. Rolling 5-game metrics (r5_*) smooth one-game noise to reveal underlying form and stabilize interpretation.

Turnovers and minutes complete the picture: turnovers end possessions, so rising TO under heavy usage is a warning sign; minutes reflect coach trust and rotation shifts, and sudden minute spikes often precede box-score jumps.

  • Efficiency (TS, eFG): shot quality converted into points; better sustainability signal than raw points.
  • usage_proxy: offensive load proxy that clarifies role and responsibility.
  • GoG deltas: immediate change detector for form, role, and availability shifts.
  • r5_* rolling: trend lens that reduces game-level randomness.
  • Turnovers (TO, gog_to): possession killers, especially concerning when paired with high usage.
  • Minutes (min, gog_min): rotation confidence indicator; spikes can foreshadow bigger stat lines.
How to read this report: Start with Stack-ranked Insights, then verify context in Top Usage, then confirm direction in Momentum Watch. Treat TS above 1.0 as a likely small-sample/outlier artifact from limited attempts or free-throw weighting.

Footnotes / Definitions

  • TS (True Shooting): PTS / (2 × (FGA + 0.44 × FTA)) — scoring efficiency adjusted for 3s and free throws; best single-number scorer efficiency proxy.
  • eFG: (FGM + 0.5 × 3PM) / FGA — shooting value per attempt, rewarding 3-point shot value above 2s.
  • usage_proxy: FGA + 0.44 × FTA + TO — approximate possessions a player consumed; proxy for offensive load.
  • GoG (game-over-game): current_stat - previous_game_stat for the same player_id — detects sudden changes game-to-game (role, matchup, injury, form).
  • r5_*: rolling_5_game_mean(stat) — reduces randomness and is usually better for reading true form.
  • min conversion: MM:SS → minutes_float
