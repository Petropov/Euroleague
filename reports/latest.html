Definitions

This report uses efficiency + role context to explain performance. Points alone are noisy; TS/eFG tell you scoring quality; usage_proxy tells you who carried possessions; GoG and r5 show change vs trend.

TS (True Shooting)

  • What it means: Scoring efficiency including 3s + free throws (single-number scorer efficiency).
  • Why it matters: Separates “volume points” from “valuable points”; more predictive than raw PTS.
  • How to read it here: TS ~ 0.55 is solid, >0.60 strong, <0.50 inefficient. TS can exceed 1.0 in tiny samples; treat TS_OUTLIER/LOW_USAGE as low confidence.
Show formula PTS / (2 × (FGA + 0.44 × FTA))

eFG (Effective FG%)

  • What it means: Shot value per attempt, giving 3s extra weight vs 2s.
  • Why it matters: Isolates shooting from free throws; useful to spot hot/cold shooting nights.
  • How to read it here: Compare eFG with TS—if TS is high but eFG is low, free throws likely drove efficiency.
Show formula (FGM + 0.5 × 3PM) / FGA

usage_proxy

  • What it means: Approximate possessions used: FGA + 0.44×FTA + TO.
  • Why it matters: Role indicator; high usage means offense ran through that player.
  • How to read it here: High usage + good TS = star-level impact. High usage + low TS (and/or HIGH_TO) = risk/drag. Low usage + high TS usually profiles as a finisher and can be small-sample sensitive.
Show formula FGA + 0.44 × FTA + TO (mirrors possession-ending events)

GoG (Game-over-Game)

  • What it means: Current stat minus previous game stat for the same player.
  • Why it matters: Detects shocks from role change, matchup, foul trouble, or injury/return.
  • How to read it here: Always pair with Confidence + Flags. ROLE_SPIKE (Δmin) suggests rotation change; USG_SPIKE suggests more on-ball responsibility.

r5_* (Rolling 5)

  • What it means: 5-game average.
  • Why it matters: Stabilizes noise and gives a better form read than one game.
  • How to read it here: When GoG and r5 move in the same direction, the trend signal is stronger.

Minutes (min)

  • What it means: Coach trust / rotation role size.
  • Why it matters: Minute spikes often precede stat spikes; minute drops can explain weak boxscores.
  • How to read it here: Trust HIGH confidence (min ≥20) more than LOW.

Turnovers (TO)

  • What it means: Possessions ended with a mistake.
  • Why it matters: High TO is costly, especially when paired with high usage.
  • How to read it here: TO 3+ is a red flag; rising gog_to under high usage signals growing risk.

Quick interpretation cheat-sheet

  • Start with Impact/Trend top 3 + Why-ranked notes.
  • Then check Top Usage to see who carried possessions.
  • If a player shows TS_OUTLIER or LOW_USAGE, treat their efficiency as low-confidence.
  • If HIGH_TO appears, downgrade performance quality even when points are high.
  • Prefer r5_* for “form”, GoG for “shock”.
